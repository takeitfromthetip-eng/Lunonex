## Our Philosophy

ForTheWeebs is committed to free expression within legal boundaries. We don't engage in arbitrary censorship, but we do enforce strict rules to comply with U.S. law, protect minors, and maintain a safe platform for creators and users.

**We believe in:**
- Creator sovereignty and artistic freedom
- Minimal interference with legal content
- Transparent enforcement with clear guidelines
- Swift action against illegal content
- Zero tolerance for CSAM (Child Sexual Abuse Material)

### For Creators
- All creators posting adult content MUST verify age with government ID
- ID documents are encrypted and stored securely
- Only authorized compliance staff can access IDs
- Failure to verify results in account suspension

### For All Performers
- Creators must verify the age of ALL performers in their content
- Maintain records per 18 U.S.C. ¬ß 2257 requirements
- Records must be kept for 7 years after content removal
- Spot audits may be conducted by ForTheWeebs compliance team

### For Users
- Mandatory age verification to access adult content

- Parental controls available for family accounts

## Enforcement & Strike System

### Content Review Process
1. **Automated Pre-Screening**: AI scans all uploads for prohibited content
2. **Flagged Content Review**: Human moderators review flagged content within 24 hours
3. **User Reports**: Community can report violations; reviewed within 48 hours
4. **Creator Appeals**: Creators can appeal decisions within 14 days

### Strike System
- **Strike 1 (Warning)**: First minor violation
  - Content removed
  - Warning issued
  - Educational resources provided

- **Strike 2 (Suspension)**: Second violation or moderate offense
  - 7-day account suspension
  - Required compliance training
  - Content review before reinstatement

- **Strike 3 (Termination)**: Third violation or severe offense
  - Permanent account termination
  - No appeal
  - IP and device ban

**Immediate Termination Offenses** (no strikes):
- CSAM or content depicting minors
- Non-consensual intimate imagery
- Human trafficking content
- Terrorist content
- Doxxing with malicious intent
- Persistent copyright infringement (DMCA)

## 2257 Compliance for Adult Content

All adult content creators MUST comply with **18 U.S.C. ¬ß 2257** record-keeping requirements:

### Required Records
- Government-issued ID for ALL performers
- Legal names, stage names, and dates of birth
- Production dates and content URLs
- Signed model release forms
- Proof of age verification (ID copies)

### Custodian of Records
- Creators must designate a Custodian of Records
- Display 2257 statement on content with custodian contact info
- Make records available for lawful inspection
- Maintain records for 7 years after content removal

### ForTheWeebs Compliance Support
- Guidance on 2257 compliance
- Templates for model releases and statements
- Random compliance audits to ensure adherence
- Immediate account termination for non-compliance

## CSAM Detection & Reporting

ForTheWeebs employs industry-leading technology to detect and prevent CSAM:

### Detection Methods
- **AI-Powered Pre-Upload Scanning**: Content analyzed before it reaches the platform
- **Hash Matching**: Comparison against NCMEC and PhotoDNA databases
- **Behavioral Analysis**: Flagging suspicious upload patterns
- **User Reports**: Community reporting with priority review

### Reporting Procedure
When CSAM is detected:
1. Content immediately removed and quarantined
2. User account permanently banned
3. IP address and device fingerprint banned
4. Report filed with **NCMEC (National Center for Missing & Exploited Children)**
5. Report filed with **FBI Cyber Crimes Division**
6. Evidence preserved for law enforcement

**We take CSAM seriously.** Attempting to upload such content will result in criminal prosecution.

## Copyright & DMCA Compliance

ForTheWeebs respects intellectual property and enforces DMCA takedown procedures.

### For Copyright Holders
- Submit DMCA takedown notices to: dmca@fortheweebs.com
- Include: copyrighted work description, infringing URL, contact info, sworn statement
- Content removed within 24 hours of valid notice

### For Creators
- Do not upload content you don't own or have rights to
- Use royalty-free or licensed music, images, and video
- Parody and fair use apply but may be challenged

### Copyright Strike System
- **1st Strike**: Content removed, warning issued
- **2nd Strike**: 7-day suspension
- **3rd Strike**: Permanent ban

**Counter-Notification**: If you believe content was wrongly removed, submit a DMCA counter-notice.

## Appeal Process

Creators who believe their content was wrongly removed can appeal:

1. **Submit Appeal**: Email appeals@fortheweebs.com within 14 days
2. **Include Details**: Content description, why it should be restored, supporting evidence
3. **Review**: Appeals reviewed by senior moderation team within 7 days
4. **Decision**: Final decision issued via email
5. **Reinstatement**: Content restored if appeal is successful

**Important:** Appeals are only for content removed in error. Violations of illegal content policies cannot be appealed.
