3. How to Submit a DMCA Takedown Notice

If you believe your copyrighted work is being infringed on ForTheWeebs, you can submit a takedown notice. Your notice must include ALL of the following information:

Required Information:

  1. Your Contact Information:
    • Full legal name
    • Email address
    • Phone number
    • Physical address
  2. Identification of Copyrighted Work:
    • Description of the copyrighted work you own
    • Registration number (if applicable)
    • Link to original work
  3. Identification of Infringing Content:
    • Direct URLs to infringing content on ForTheWeebs
    • Specific description of what is infringing
  4. Good Faith Statement:

    "I have a good faith belief that use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law."

  5. Accuracy Statement:

    "The information in this notification is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, I am authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed."
