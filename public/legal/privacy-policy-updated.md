## What Data We Collect

- Stored securely with Stripe (we never see your full credit card number)

### **Usage Data**
- Pages you visit on our platform

- Error logs (to fix bugs)

### **Technical Data**
- IP address (for security and fraud prevention)

**What we DON'T collect:**
- We don't track you across other websites
- We don't monitor your browsing outside our platform
- We don't scan your private messages for advertising
- We don't build "shadow profiles"
